High5! App

Using Node/Express/MySql/Handlebars

High5! Creates a shared experience of unique cultural practices of different online affinity networks, and how they interact with one another brings together similar interests, social activities, accomplishments, and career relevant arenas. For some an online community is a part of growing up in the networked age. This app will help you find a close-knit group of people that you can vent to about your problems or share your excitement about your team winning the championship.
